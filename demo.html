Overview

With the emergence of a new era of thriving entrepreneurship, the B.B.A. as an undergraduate programme has earned a well-deserved reputation for equipping students with essential business knowledge. Certainly, the Bachelor of Business Administration programme at Indira College of Commerce and Science is meant to give students a comprehensive understanding of a company's functional areas and their relationships. As a result, B.B.A. students are exposed to a wide range of basic topics. This programme teaches pupils entrepreneurship and business management skills

Indira College of Commerce and Science is one of the best colleges for gaining entrepreneurial practical skills along with a rigorous and up-to-date theoretical curriculum

The emphasis is on skill development and research orientation so that students are prepared to work in an entrepreneurial environment or pursue higher education at prestigious business schools. Students have the option of receiving a standard B.B.A. or a speciality B.B.A. award.
